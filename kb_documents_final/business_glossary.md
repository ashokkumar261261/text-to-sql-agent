# Business Glossary and Terminology

## Customer Management
- **Customer**: A business entity that purchases products or services from the company
- **Customer ID**: Unique identifier for each customer in the system
- **Customer Name**: The official business name or individual name of the customer
- **Customer Email**: Primary contact email address for the customer
- **Customer Phone**: Primary contact phone number for the customer
- **Customer Address**: Physical or mailing address of the customer
- **Signup Date**: The date when the customer first registered or was added to the system
- **Customer Status**: Current status of the customer (active, inactive, suspended)
- **Customer Type**: Classification of customer (individual, business, enterprise)

## Product Management
- **Product**: An item or service offered for sale by the company
- **Product ID**: Unique identifier for each product in the catalog
- **Product Name**: The official name or title of the product
- **Product Description**: Detailed description of the product features and benefits
- **Product Category**: Classification group that the product belongs to
- **Product Price**: The current selling price of the product
- **Product Cost**: The cost to produce or acquire the product
- **Product Status**: Current availability status (active, discontinued, out of stock)
- **SKU**: Stock Keeping Unit - unique product identifier for inventory management

## Order Management
- **Order**: A request from a customer to purchase one or more products
- **Order ID**: Unique identifier for each order transaction
- **Order Date**: The date when the order was placed by the customer
- **Order Status**: Current state of the order (pending, processing, shipped, delivered, cancelled)
- **Order Total**: The total monetary value of the order including all items and fees
- **Order Quantity**: The number of items ordered
- **Shipping Address**: The address where the order should be delivered
- **Payment Method**: The method used to pay for the order (credit card, PayPal, etc.)
- **Tracking Number**: Unique identifier for shipment tracking

## Financial Terms
- **Revenue**: Total income generated from sales of products or services
- **Gross Revenue**: Total revenue before any deductions
- **Net Revenue**: Revenue after deductions for returns, discounts, and allowances
- **Profit**: Revenue minus costs and expenses
- **Margin**: The difference between selling price and cost, often expressed as a percentage
- **Commission**: Payment made to sales representatives based on sales performance
- **Discount**: Reduction in price offered to customers
- **Tax**: Government-imposed charge on transactions
- **Refund**: Money returned to customer for returned or cancelled orders

## Sales and Marketing
- **Lead**: A potential customer who has shown interest in products or services
- **Conversion**: When a lead becomes a paying customer
- **Campaign**: Marketing initiative designed to promote products or generate leads
- **Channel**: The method or platform through which sales are made (online, retail, partner)
- **Territory**: Geographic or market segment assigned to a sales representative
- **Quota**: Sales target assigned to sales representatives or teams
- **Pipeline**: The collection of potential sales opportunities in various stages
- **Churn**: The rate at which customers stop doing business with the company

## Inventory and Supply Chain
- **Inventory**: Stock of products available for sale
- **Stock Level**: Current quantity of a product in inventory
- **Reorder Point**: Inventory level that triggers a new order from suppliers
- **Supplier**: Company or individual that provides products or materials
- **Warehouse**: Physical location where inventory is stored
- **Shipment**: Delivery of products from one location to another
- **Backorder**: Order for a product that is temporarily out of stock
- **Lead Time**: Time required to receive products from suppliers

## Performance Metrics
- **KPI**: Key Performance Indicator - metrics used to measure business performance
- **ROI**: Return on Investment - measure of investment efficiency
- **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)**: Total revenue expected from a customer over their relationship
- **Average Order Value (AOV)**: Average monetary value of orders
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: Cost to acquire a new customer
- **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)**: Predictable revenue generated each month
- **Churn Rate**: Percentage of customers who stop using the service in a given period
- **Conversion Rate**: Percentage of leads that become customers

## Time Periods and Reporting
- **Fiscal Year**: 12-month period used for financial reporting and budgeting
- **Quarter**: Three-month period (Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4)
- **Month-to-Date (MTD)**: From the beginning of the current month to the current date
- **Year-to-Date (YTD)**: From the beginning of the current year to the current date
- **Same Period Last Year (SPLY)**: Corresponding period in the previous year
- **Rolling 12 Months**: The most recent 12-month period
- **Seasonality**: Predictable changes in business patterns based on time of year
- **Trend**: General direction of change in metrics over time
