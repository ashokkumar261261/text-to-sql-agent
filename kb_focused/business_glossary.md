# Business Glossary

## Customer Metrics
- **Top customers by revenue**: Customers ranked by total amount spent (SUM of total_amount from orders)
- **Customer lifetime value**: Total revenue generated by a customer across all orders
- **Active customers**: Customers who have placed orders recently
- **At-risk customers**: Customers who haven't ordered in a long time

## Order Metrics  
- **Revenue**: Sum of total_amount from delivered orders
- **Order count**: Number of orders placed
- **Average order value**: Average total_amount per order

## Time Periods
- **Recent**: Within last 30-90 days
- **At risk**: No orders in 90+ days
- **Current date**: Use CURRENT_DATE in queries

## Status Values
- Orders can have status: 'Delivered', 'Shipped', 'Processing'
- Only count 'Delivered' orders for revenue calculations
